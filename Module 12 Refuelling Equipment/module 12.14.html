Session 12.14

Refueller Tank

Coupler Lift Assist Device (CLAD)

    CLAD should comply with EI 1576 Hydrant pit coupler lift assist device specifications

  • Pneumatically operated lift arm to aid the loading and unloading of the hydrant coupler
  • Pneumatic clamp system to unhook hydrant coupler
  • Easily accessible pneumatic operating controls for lifting arm and grippers actuation
  • Hooking elements to couple CALD to vehicle
  • Integral hydrant pit flag holder
  • Retractable handle for easy transport

Refueller Tank

  • Aluminium alloy or stainless steel or mild steel internally coated with a light-coloured epoxy material compatible with aviation fuels
  • Tanks for fuel mixed with FSII shall be stainless steel only and not aluminium or lined mild steel
  • Single compartment with anti-surge baffles
  • Tank contents gauge
  • Bottom loading through a self-sealing (tight fit) connection
  • Combination of one automatic overfill prevention device and one automatic cut-off device, one connected to the foot valve and one connected to the loading line valve with a pre-check
  • Pump round connections
  • Completely self-draining with 1” low point drain labelled low point sump, with a drain line and valve
  • 3% volume to be allowed for product expansion
  • Manhole fittings to incorporate p&v breathers
  • Venting to be sized to accommodate maximum loading and delivery flow rates
  • Manhole rollover protection to be provided
  • Tank top drains to be external to tank
  • tank top access appropriate fall protection, ladder and non-slip tank top walkway to be fitted
  • Semi-trailers to be provided with landing legs

12.14.2 Overfill protection System

  • Overfill protection System should consists of a combination of one automatic independent overfill prevention device (alarm or cut-off) and one independent fuel cut-off device
  • Two sensors, one connected to the foot valve and one connected to the loading line valve and set to stop the flow at predetermined levels
  • The higher of the two devices provides the ultimate automatic overfill prevention, commonly referred to as the “high-high” level
  • Ultimate device shall be fitted with a pre-check function that shall be activated shortly after the start of each loading
  • Electronic tank gauging Programmable safe fill level (primary overfill protection if required)
